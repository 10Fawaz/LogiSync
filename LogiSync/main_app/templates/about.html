About LogiSync

LogiSync is a logistics management platform designed to streamline the tracking, coordination, and delivery of goods across complex supply chains. Whether you're managing containers, packages, or transport routes, LogiSync helps you stay organized, efficient, and in control.

What We Offer

  • Container Tracking: Monitor container locations, weights, and descriptions in real time.
  • Package Management: Create, update, and view packages with detailed metadata including owner, weight, and received date.
  • Transport Coordination: Assign transports to destinations and sources, manage capacity, and upload transport images for reference.
  • User-Specific Views: Each user sees only their own data, ensuring privacy and clarity across teams.

Why LogiSync?

We built LogiSync to solve the everyday pain points of logistics professionals — from scattered spreadsheets to unclear responsibilities. With secure authentication, per-user data filtering, and a clean interface, LogiSync empowers teams to collaborate without confusion.

Built for Developers, Too

LogiSync is also a developer-friendly platform. Built with Django and PostgreSQL, it features scalable architecture, clean models, and maintainable CRUD flows. Whether you're customizing the backend or extending the frontend, LogiSync is designed to grow with your needs.

Get Started

If you're new here, feel free to sign up or log in to explore your dashboard. If you're already part of the team, welcome back — your logistics universe awaits.
